About

It's hackening! The University of Hawaii's and our beloved sponsors are ramping up to Summer 2013 with a hackathon, May 13-15, when the students of the UH systems will convene, collaborate, and manifest the real Big Ideas--or fail gloriously in trying.

Like Startup Weekend Honolulu and City Camp, this competition will focus on pitching ideas, forming teams, and building a software application in under 50 hours. The entries will be judged by software industry professionals, and prizes will be awarded.

Get Ready

We will be hosting weekly workshops on Wednesday of each week (see schedule below) leading up to the start of the hackathon (May 13th). The topic of each workshop may vary from technology tutorials, to structured presentations from select speakers. Top technologists, educators and business leaders have volunteered not only as judges in the main event, but also as capacity-building mentors to all student-participants.

For questions like "How do I participate?" see the FAQ.
